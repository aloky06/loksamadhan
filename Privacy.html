Privacy Policy



This privacy policy explains how “Lok Samadhan” collects, uses, and protects any information that you provide when making a donation on our website. “Lok Samadhan” is committed to ensuring that your privacy is safeguarded. Should we ask you to provide any information through which you can be identified when using this website, rest assured that it will only be used in accordance with this privacy statement. We may update this policy from time to time by modifying this page. You should review this page periodically to ensure you are happy with any changes. This policy is effective from 2nd Oct 2025.

Information We Collect

When you make a donation, we may collect the following information:

Name
Contact information, including email address and phone number
Billing address, including postcode
Payment information (e.g., credit card details, bank account details for electronic transfers)
Demographic information such as preferences and interests
Other information relevant to donor preferences, feedback, and surveys

How We Use the Information

We require this information to process your donations and keep you updated on the impact of your contribution. Specifically, we may use your information for:
Processing Donations: Safely and securely processing your financial contributions to our organization.
Sending Donation Receipts: Providing you with a receipt and confirmation of your donation.
Internal Record Keeping: Maintaining accurate records for auditing and legal purposes.
Communications: Sending updates about our projects, campaigns, and ongoing initiatives you may be interested in.
Improving Our Programs: Using feedback from donors to enhance the effectiveness of our programs and outreach.

Payment Security

We take the security of your payment information seriously. All transactions are encrypted and processed using secure methods. We do not store your credit card or payment details after your transaction is completed.

Data Retention

We retain donor information as long as necessary for the purposes outlined above or as required by law. If you wish to modify or delete your personal information, please contact us, and we will act upon your request as per applicable regulations.

How We Share Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except as required for processing your donations, complying with legal obligations, or in connection with your consent.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. If you wish to opt-out of any communications, or for any data-related inquiries, please contact us.
